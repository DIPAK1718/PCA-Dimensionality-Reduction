Q: Why do we use imshow() in image datasets?
Answer:
imshow() is used to visualize image data stored as numerical pixel values. It converts a matrix of pixel intensities into a human-readable image.

Why do we use cumulative variance?

Answer:
It helps determine the minimum number of principal components required to retain a desired amount of information, such as 90% or 95%.
